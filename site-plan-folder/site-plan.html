Ultra Concrete LLC – Site Plan

WDD 131 Dynamic Web Fundamentals

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help people learn about Ultra Concrete LLC and the concrete services we offer. It also provides a quick way for users to get a simple estimate and check weather conditions for concrete pouring. The goal is to make the process easy for homeowners and builders.

Audience

The audience includes homeowners, contractors, and real estate developers who need concrete work. They want clear information, examples of past projects, and quick estimates. They also need an easy way to contact the company.

Branding

Logo

The logo will be simple and construction-focused, with bold text and a small concrete-related icon. It will use dark gray and orange colors to match a professional concrete brand.

Style Guide

Color Palette

The main colors will be dark gray, orange, white, and light gray. These colors match construction work and keep the site clean and readable.

Dynamic Elements

The site will have a simple estimate calculator where users enter the size of their concrete project and get an estimated price. It will also show basic weather warnings that help users know if pouring concrete might be risky. JavaScript will update the page, handle user input, and show different messages based on conditions.

Content

Home Page Content

The home page will introduce the company, list the main services, and show a button leading to the estimate calculator. It will also include at least one image and simple navigation.

Child Page Content (Estimates & Services)

The child page will include the full estimate tool, weather warnings, and more details about services. It will guide users to request a formal quote after using the tools.

Wireframes

Home Page Wireframe (Description)

The home page will have a header with the logo, a main section with a short introduction, and a list of services. Below that will be a button linking to the estimate page, and the footer will show contact information.

Child Page Wireframe (Description)

The child page will start with a title, followed by the estimate calculator form. Under that will be a small weather warning section and then a list of services. The footer will match the home page.
